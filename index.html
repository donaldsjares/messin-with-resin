Our Story

Born from curiosity,
made with passion.

What started as a simple curiosity about resin art quickly blossomed into our full-blown creative passion. Since 2023, Messin With Resin has taken over our Central Texas home — two bedrooms and counting.

We occasionally build our own molds to bring your wildest ideas to life. Nothing is ever mass produced. Every single piece is poured and finished by hand.
